ABOUT US

Welcome to Kripa Power Solutions

Founded in 2000 by a highly innovative team, Kripa Power Solutions designs, develops, tests and implements a diverse range of products comprising the full cycle from concept to deployment.Kripa Power Solutions is uniquely positioned to offer an array of world-class products and solutions to meet all hardware requirements of telecom, lighting and renewable energy industry.The engagements span across multiple locations in India & few other countries and with over 100 R&D man-years of experience.

Kripa Power Solutions is a leading manufacturer in the high potential LED / Lighting industry. The portfolio of products also includes Radio Frequency & Antenna equipment, Surveillance & Telecom products and solar industry solutions.The brand’s distinct association with “quality” as a default feature helps in great natural differentiation in comparison to the other products in the market. The organization has been the primary business vendor to many established brands for over a decade.

Kripa's phenomenal success can be directly linked to the company’s customer centric operational strategies. Customers and Businesses are assured innovation, cost-effective solutions of superior quality with zero defects in the shortest possible time.Our foray of quality recognitions are an embodiment to our distinguishing and acute focus towards both our mission and customer satisfaction.
