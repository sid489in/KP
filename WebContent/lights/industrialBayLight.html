INDUSTRIAL BAY LIGHT

High Bay Light (LED Warehouse Light, LED Industrial Light) are designed to replace traditional H.I.D. (High Intensity Discharge) lamps. Metal Halide High Bay Fixtures, one of the families of HID lamps are used for general purpose lighting including warehouse facilities, assembly areas, gyms, hangars, transportation garages, loading and staging areas.

LED high bay lights are the ideal replacements of metal halide high bay lamps, as LED high bay lights can save about 50 - 75% of energy comparing with metal halide lamps. The replacement of LED high bay lights can not only save the energy and electricity bills, it can also lower the maintenance fees as LED's lifespan is 5 to 10 times longer than traditioal H.I.D lamps.
