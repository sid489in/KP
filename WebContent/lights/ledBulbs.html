LED BULBS

LED light bulbs are made by arranging identical light emitting diodes closely to form the shape of a light bulb. The visible light thus produced closely resembles day light and is very capable of replacing the traditional light bulbs such as incandescent lightbulbs and compact fluorescent lamps.Energy-saving light bulbs can be used in indoor and outdoor.
